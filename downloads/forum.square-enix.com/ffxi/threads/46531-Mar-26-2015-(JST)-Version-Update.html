    Event-related

    • Mog Gardens have undergone the following adjustments.
      • Monster Rearing has undergone the following adjustments.
        • A monster's mood will no longer degrade from "beaming with pure contentment" after collecting items or interacting with the monster.
        • The collective moods of all creatures will generate auras of light or darkness that affects them all.
          • Light
            When the number of creatures beaming with pure contentment is greater than that succumbing to darkness, all creatures’ contentment and mood are increased.
          • Darkness
            When the number of creatures succumbing to darkness is greater than that beaming with pure contentment, all monsters' contentment and mood are decreased.
      • The following information is now displayed when entering your Mog Garden.
        • The number of days left on assistants’ contracts.
          The number of days left on each assistant’s contract is now be announced to the player, and the timing of the announcement may be changed by speaking with the Green Thumb Moogle.
          The following three settings are possible.
          • Always announce contract information regardless of time remaining.
          • Announce when there is one day remaining, seven days remaining, or any number of days in between.
          • Do not announce time remaining.
        • Current affinity
      • If an assistant’s contract has previously expired when interacting with a gathering point, a message to that effect now displays.
      • If an assistant’s contract expires while the player is still in their Mog Garden, speaking to the assistant will cause them to disappear.
      • When entering another player’s Mog Garden, the invited player will no longer occasionally earn extra shining stars.
        Shining stars may be earned one per day by entering one’s own Mog Garden.
      • It is now possible to scrap any unwanted flotsam directly without receiving it in cases where the player’s inventory is full or he is otherwise unable to recover it.
    • Unity Concord has undergone the following additions and adjustments.
      • New Wanted objectives have been added.
        • Areas and notorious monsters
          The Labyrinth of Onzozo
          Voso
          Garlaige Citadel
          Mephitas
          Ifrit’s Cauldron
          Coca
          The Boyahda Tree
          Ayapec
          Kuftal’s Tunnel
          Specter Worm
          Den of Rancor
          Azrael
          Fei’Yin
          Orientalis Shadow／Borealis Shadow／
          Australis Shadow／Occidentalis Shadow
          *Four notorious monsters have been added to the same objective in Fei'Yin, and each must be defeated for successful completion.
        A variety of equipment is available as rewards for vanquishing these NMs.
      • Adventurers in parties of between three and five characters may now call forth alter egos in Wanted battles.
        * A party of at least three players is required to battle notorious monsters.
      • A new option to spend Unity accolades to travel to an area for which the player has accepted a Wanted objective will be added.
        Selecting the option “Currently accepted area” will display areas for which the player has accepted a Wanted objective.
        *Areas from expansions that have not yet been installed will not be displayed.
    • Records of Eminence has undergone the following additions and adjustments.
      • New objectives have been added.
      • A capacity point bonus has been added to successful first time completion of certain objectives. Characters who have already completed these objectives will have the bonus retroactively applied.
      • The Unity and Achievement categories have switched locations.
      • Other characters are no longer displayed while talking to Records of Eminence NPCs in order to ease graphical burdens.
    • The items required for the quest “Choosing an Automaton Frame” have changed.
      • Valoredge X-900
        Now requires patas instead of tigerfangs.
      • Sharpshot Z-500

      * In accordance with this change, geomancer and rune fencer quests will become temporarily unable to be undertaken again as of the March version update. A different method for attempting them again will be added in a subsequent version update.

    Battle-related

    • New battle content known as Vagary has been added.
      • What is Vagary?
        Vagary is a new type of battle content that pits pioneers against members of the Xol Triumvirate, sinister forces that made their first appearance in the Seekers of Adoulin mission storyline. Engimatic gates found within Outer Ra’Kaznar connect to otherworldly realms where adventurers and their allies face off against Balamor, Dhokmak the Brash, and Ashrakk Duskbrood.
        • Requirements for Participation
          • Have installed and registered the Seekers of Adoulin expansion disk
          • Be of level 95 or higher
          • Be in a party or alliance of between three and eighteen players
          • The party leader must have progressed to a certain point in the Seekers of Adoulin mission stoyline*
          • The party leader must be in possession of the requisite key item.*
            * Party members other than the leader need not fulfill this prerequisite, but Seekers of Adoulin lore is deeply intertwined with Vagary content. Those who wish to enjoy the story without spoilers are advised to complete the missions first.
        • Obtaining the requisite key items
          Players can obtain the necessary key items by trading vials of befouled water dropped by fomors in Outer Ra’Kaznar and Ra’Kaznar Inner Court to the odyssean passage found in Leafallia.
        • Entering Vagary
          Gates that tie Ulbuka to the otherworldly locations where Vagary takes place can be found in Outer Ra’Kaznar. There are three such gates, and the layout of this extradimensional realm and the member of the triumvirate found within differ accordingly.
          * The key items required for entry are the same for all three gates.
        • Conquering Vagary
          Vagary has a time limit of forty-five minutes. Pioneers must vanquish the threats that lie within and keep pressing forward until they come face-to-face with one of the triumvirate. Those victorious will be rewarded with handsome spoils.
        • Vagary rewards
          Besting the goes within Vagary will reward players with item level 119 equipment, materials to synthesize Superior 2 equipment, and items used to reforge empyrean armor to item level 119.

    System-related

    • /lockstyle functionality has been changed.
      • A category for locking/unlocking character equipment appearance has been added to the Misc. 3 section of the Config submenu.
        • Style Lock
          This category has the same effect as the /lockstyle commands.
          Selecting “Static” will lock the character’s appearance.
      • The conditions for a character’s appearance to be automatically unlocked have been changed to the following
        • When changing jobs.
        • When becoming the victim of a monster ability that removes the player’s equipment.
          * When changing the main hand weapon to a main hand weapon of a different type, only the weapon graphic will change.
          In relation to the above, if the player switches their main weapon to that of a different weapon type and then switching back to a weapon of the original type, the /lockstyle functionality will reactivate and display the appearance of the originally set weapon. For example, if a player has their weapon appearance locked at Sword A and then equips a club, only the weapon graphic will change from sword A to the newly equipped club. If the player then switches out the club for Sword B, the lockstyle functionality will reactivate and the graphic for sword A will display.
      • New functions have been added to the /lockstyle text command.
        • A Style Lock category has been added to the Edit Equipment Set section of the Macros submenu
          Players may lock in their equipment appearance by registering an equipment set, selecting it, and then choosing Style Lock.
        • Method for Fixing Appearance
          Players may select which equipment set they would like to have displayed by using the /lockstyleset command together with the number of a registered equipment set.
          • /lockstyleset [equipment set number]
            Sets your character’s appearance to be that of the designated equipment set. Not designating a number will display the currently equipped items. This command may also be used while appearance is currently locked.

            Ex. “/lockstyleset 1” will fix the character’s appearance at that of the equipment designated in equipment set 1.
          * This command may only be executed once every ten seconds in order to reduce server load.
        • Method for unlocking equipment apperance
          Appearance may be unlocked by turning off the option from the config menu or using the /lockstyle command once more.
        Read on for information about equipment sets.
        • The conditions for which equipment may be used with the style lock functionality have been changed.
          If these conditions are fulfilled, players are able to display even equipment that is not usable by their current job. The new conditions for /lockstyle are as follows.
          • The player must be in possession of the piece.
          • The player must fulfill the conditions to equip the piece on at least one job they have acquired.
          • For weapons, the weapon type for main and sub weapons must be the same as those currently equipped.
          Ex. A player in possession of Burtgang, paladin relic armor, and a full set of Orvail armor has paladin at level 99 and red mage at level 30 and sets their main job to be red mage.

          * Burtgang/paladin relic armor
          The player is able to change their job to paladin and meets the level requirement to equip the items, so they can be used with the style lock functionality.

          * Orvail armor
          The player’s red mage level is insufficient to equip the orvail armor, so it cannot be used with the style lock functionality.

          * When equipping weapons other than swords
          The paladin relic armor will remain fixed, but the Burgang will not.
    • Arcane glyptics engraved by Divainy-Gamainy or Detrovio in Western Adoulin (J-10) will now appear at the top of the item help text.
    • Linkshell messages from both primary and secondary linkshells are now displayed upon logging in.
    • A text command to display sublinkshell messages has been added.
      • /linkshell2mes, /ls2mes
        Allows the player to view their current sublinkshell group’s welcome message.
      * Players must set a linkshell to be their primary one in order to edit these messages.
    • Players are now able to use the /recast text command to display the recast timer for abilities that consume charges.
    • The “Friend List” button in the main menu has been renamed “Communication.”
      The existing friend list functions remain the same, and a new category, “Emote List,” enables the player to perform a variety of emotes directly from the menu.
    • A new category, “Area Display” has been added to the Misc. 3 section of the Config submenu.
      * This category is currently inactive, and the functionality will be added in a subsequent version update.
    • The log window has undergone the following adjustments.
