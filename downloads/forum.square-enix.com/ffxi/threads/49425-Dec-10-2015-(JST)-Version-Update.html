    Event-related

    • A new quest related to Aeonic weapons has been added.
      Aeonic weapons are a new class of weapons alongside Relic, Mythic, Empyrean, and Ergon weapons.
      These weapons allow their bearers to use weapon skills obtained through the power of the bushin and grant and Aftermath effect when used. While this Aftermath effect is active, the wielder can execute the ultimate Radiance and Umbra skillchains.
      *You must have completed the final chapter of Rhapsodies of Vana'diel to receive this quest.
      *The strength of used weapon skills is equivalent to that of a fifth tier upgraded merit point category.
      *While the Aftermath effect is active, applicable skillchains will be imbued with either Light or Darkness.
      *If an ultimate skill chain is successfully executed, the Aftermath effect will be lost.
    • The following additions have been made to Unity Concord.
      • New Unity shirts have been added.
        If you have an individual evaluation of 10 points or greater for two weeks running, you will be able to purchase a unique outfit styled after your Unity leader from your Unity's item exchange list.
        Calling forth your Unity leader's alter ego while your Unity shirt is equipped will grant you status effect bonuses that scale with your Unity's ranking.
        *Speak to either Urbiolaine in Southern San d'Oria (G-10), Igsli in Bastok Markets (E-11), Teldro-Kesdrodo Yonolala in Windurst Woods (J-10), or Nunaarl Bthtrogg in Western Adoulin (H-11).
        *You can only claim the Unity shirt of your current Unity.
      • During Wanted battles, unrelated PCs, NPCs, and monsters will be hidden from view.
    • A new jump emote has been added.
      A player character can now leap into the air by selecting "/jump" from the emote list or by entering the "/jump" text command.
      *This emote cannot be used to jump to raised platforms or otherwise access new areas.
      *The jump emote may be interrupted, for instance, when receiving damage.
      • When can I jump?
        The /jump emote can be used together with the following actions:
        Standing still / Walking / Running / Sliding / Moving backwards / Sheathing a weapon / Drawing a weapon
    • Item exchange NPCs will now handle the following items.
      Sarissaphoroi belt / Luminary sash / Tatenashi gote / Tatenashi gote +1 / Thureous earring /
      Dignitary's earring / Exemplar / Tatenashi sune-ate / Tatenashi sune-ate +1 / Sanctity necklace / Solemnity cape
    • New titles have been added.
