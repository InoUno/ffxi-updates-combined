    Event-related

    • New quests have been added.
      • The Weatherspoon War
        Players must speak to Zaffeld in Eastern Adoulin (J-8) after meeting the following requirements in order to receive the quest.
        • Have completed the quest The Weatherspoon Inquisition.
        • Be of Adoulin fame rank 3 or greater.
        The final showdown with the Velkk is nigh. Will the exorcists and the Blackthorn Coven be able to set aside their differences to vanquish that which threatens them both?
      • A Thirst Before Time
        Players must speak to Roskin in Eastern Adoulin (H-8) after meeting the following requirements in order to receive the quest.
        • Have completed the quest A Thirst for Eternity and have Roskin's volume available for viewing in Celennia Memorial Library.
        • Have completed the quest The Curious Case of Melvien.
        A demon in black and red makes a fateful appearance that will alter the course of several Adoulinians' lives, while a hidden part of the Scouts' Coalition's maester Margret's past will finally be brought to light
      • Thanks for All the Fish
        Players must speak to Jourdenaux in Rabao (F-8) after meeting the following requirements in order to receive the quest.
        • Be in possession of Lu Shang's fishing rod.
        • Have completed the Records of Eminence objective First Step Forward
        Just who is this perverted old man, and why does he want all these fish?
      • Fish Favor the Bold
        Players must speak to Irmilant in Rabao (G-7) after meeting the following requirements in order to receive the quest.
        • Be in possession of the Ebisu Fishing Rod
        • Have fished up at least 100 types of catches by one's own hand*
          * Only catches reeled in after the June 2015 version update will be counted.
        • Have installed and registered the following content.
          Rise of the Zilart / Chains of Promathia / Treasures of Aht Urhgan / Wings of the Goddess / Seekers of Adoulin / Scars of Abyssea
        An exalted fisherman has come to offer a challenge!
      • Doctor Chacharoon
        Players must enter their Mog Garden after meeting the following requirements in order to receive the quest.
        • Be in possession of the "Sakura's Excellent Adventure" key item.
    • The difficult of the following quests has been adjusted.
      • The Gustaberg Tour
        Players must now be in a party of two characters below level 15 instead of six characters below level 5 to complete the quest.
        * Alter egos and fellows do not count toward the requirements.
      • The Kuftal Tour
        Players must now be in a party of two characters below level 40 instead of six characters below level 40 to complete the quest.
        * Alter egos and fellows do not count toward the requirements.
      • The "Eco-Warrior" series of quests.
        • Alter egos may now be called forth.
        • The level limitation has been increased from 20 to 25.
        • The NPC at the dungeon entrance will now describe where the goal is located.
      • The quest log entries for Wrath of the Opo-opos and Wandering Souls have been adjusted.
      • The Warhors Footprints in the quests Promotion: Superior Private and Promotion: Corporal now appear on wide scan and have visible effects to make them better stand out.
    • Mog Houses have undergone the following adjustments.
      • The "Select an area to exit to" option that displays when exiting one's Mog House now displays at the bottom of the dialog box.
      • The "None for now" option that displays when interacting with the Symphonic Curator now displays at the top of the dialog box.
    • Mog Gardens have undergone the following adjustments.
      • Monster Rearing rank can now be increased to 5, and has undergone the following additions and adjustments.
        • Two new types of creatures may be reared.
        • New Chacharoon Cheer effects have been added.
        • Up to three creatures may be reared at once.
        • The following items may now be given to creatures as feed.
          Blue Pondweed / Red Pondweed / Date / Royal Grape / Burdock / Popoto / Walnut / Yellow Ginseng /
          Elixir / Dragon Meat / Tavnazian Liver / Bibiki Urchin / Dragon Fruit / Kitron / Persikos / Reishi Mushroom /
          Agaricus / Coral Fungus / Sobbing Fungus
    • Escha – Zi'Tah has undergone the following additions and adjustments.
      • Experience point acquisition while in a party has been adjusted to be the same as in Seekers of Adoulin areas. As a result, experience point gains while in a party have been increased.
      • A new key items that will increase the maximum amount of Escha silt and beads that may be held at once has been added. This item may be purchased with Escha silt.

    System-related

    • Players are now able to fix the appearance of their ranged weapons using the style lock functionality.
    • The /sitchair text command has been added.
      This command enables players to lean their backs against the chair. Entering the command while seated will cause the player to stand up.

      Subcommand
      1/BlankWooden Stool
      1Imperial Chair
      2Decorative Chair
      3Ornate Stool
      4Refined Chair
      onSit on the designated chair
      offStand up
      * The appropriate key items are required to sit in chairs one through four.
      * Players will automatically stand up when they move or are damaged in much the same way as with the /sit command.
      • It is possible select this emote from the emote list as well.
      • The following emotes may be used in while sitting using /sitchair
        Nod your head. (/yes,/nod)
        Shake your head. (/no)
        Wave to your target. (/wave,/farewell,/goodbye)
        Shrug in confusion. (/huh)
        Laugh out loud. (/laugh)
        Begin to cry. (/cry)
        Clap your hands. (/clap)
    • The /targetcharaeffect text command has been added.
      Displays a visual indicator of targets that will be affected by spells and abilities center on the specified target.
      Subcommand
      BlankToggle between on and off
      onEnables display of visual indicators
      offDisables display of visual indicators
      Visual indicators are display in the same font size regardless of distance.
    • The /bstpet text command has been added.
      Performs the beastmaster pet command Ready. Displays the index numbers of all usable pet abilities if not subcommand is specified. Specific abilities have accompanying index numbers that may be designated in place of the ability name.
      Subcommand
      Ability namePerforms the designated ability.
      Index numberPerforms the designated ability.
      BlankDisplays ability names along with the appropriate index number.

    Known Issues

    • Players will occasionally become stuck on terrain in certain areas of Sih Gates.
    • In the high-tier battlefield The Savage, spoils will occasionally fail to drop if a familiar delivers the killing blow.
    • When fishing, the player will appear to stop moving when reeling in a fish for an extending period of time would .
    • Healer-type alter egos will occasionally stop curing status ailments when a party member is knocked out.
    • In the Eco-Warrior series of quests, alter egos will occasionally not be dismissed when level restrictions are removed.
      * Alter egos who fail to be dismissed in thie way will retain their lower level attributes. Dismissing them manually and calling them forth again will resolve this issue.

    PlayStation 2 Only
    • The screen will black out and become unresponsive in the quest In a Haze of Glory.
