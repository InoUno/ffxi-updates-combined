    Event-related

    • The final chapter in the Seekers of Adoulin mission has been added.
      Players completing the mission will be rewarded with three special items.
      • The councilor's garb chestpiece
      • The councilor's cuffs handwear
      • One of twelve different rings
      • Councilor's Garb

        * The actual defense of the item is 1, not 51 as displayed in the screenshot.
      • Councilor's Cuffs
      • Choice of one of the twelve different rings given below.
        * Players misplacing their rewards should speak with Ploh Trishbahk in Eastern Adoulin (K-9) to receive new ones.
        * Players wishing to exchange their ring for one of a different type need only speak to Ploh Trishbahk and pay a fee of 300,000 bayld.
        * The help text for the Vocane ring erroneously reads “Damage taken II -3%” instead of “Damage taken -7%.”
    • A new area, Ra'Kaznar Turris, has been added.
    • New sections of existing areas have been added.
      These areas can be accessed by possessing a particular key item.
      Area Required Key Item
      Sih Gates Aged Matriarch Naakual Crest
      Moh Gates Aged Firebrand Naakual Crest
      Woh Gates Aged Booming Naakual Crest
      Dho Gates Aged Riptide Naakual Crest
      Cirdas Caverns Aged Ligneous Naakual Crest
    • A new entrance to Outer Ra'Kaznar from Woh Gates has been discovered.
      This entrance may only be used by those in possession of the aged flashfrost naakual crest key item.
    • A new system known as “Unity Concord” has been added.
      Read on for details.
    • Mog Gades have undergone the following additions and adjustments.
      • New fertilizer has been added for purchase from the Green Thumb Moogle.
      • The effects of the active fertilizer will now display when selecting a gathering point.
      • When gathering multiple times from an arboreal grove or mineral vein, the gathering attempts will stop partway if the effects of the fertilizer disappear.
      • Monster Rearing has undergone the following additions and adjustments.
        • A second rank is now available for monster rearing.
          Players must purchase a copy of the Sakura and the Magic Spoon key item from one of the following NPCs to increase their monster rearing rank.
          Skipper Moogles in Port Sand d'Oria (I-8), Port Bastok (K-11), or Port Windurst (C-8) / Zennica in Western Adoulin (E-9)
        • New monsters are available for monster rearing.
          * Players must increase their Monster Rearing skill to rank two in order to raise these monsters.
        • The following items may now be fed to monsters.
          Cockatrice Meat / Quus / Coral Butterfly / Noble Lady
    • Fishing nodes have been added to Sih Gates.
    • The following NPCs directing players to local quests have been added.
      Ombelotte in Tavnazian Safehold (H-7) / Esteban in Selbina (I-8) / Marilionne in Mhaura (H-7)
    • Information on Records of Eminence and the Trust initiative can now be obtained from the following NPCs.
      Ailevia in Southern San d'Oria (I-9) / Matildie in Northern San d'Oria (J-8) / Teilsa in Port San d'Oria (F-9) / Arva in Bastok Mines (H-7) / Reet in Bastok Markets (G-8) / Dulsie in Port Bastok (J-8) / Jack of Hearts in Windurst Waters (G-9) / Jack of Diamonds in Windurst Walls (C-13) / Jack of Clubs in Port Windurst (E-6) / Jack of Spades in Windurst Woods (H-11)
    • Chocobo Racing has undergone the following adjustments.
      • It is now possible to skip the cutscenes accompanying completed official races and free runs.
      • The maximum cost for participating in free runs has been increased.
        * The cost will continue to reset to midnight (JST) as previously.
      • Prizes will no longer be awarded for novices races I-III if those races have already been completed previously.
        * Players will continue to receive rewards for their first clear.

    System-related

    • New NPCs known as Linkshell Concierges have been added.
      • Linkshell Concierges
        Linkshell concierges are NPCs that match linkshells looking for players with players looking for linkshells. Any player with a linkshell or pearlsack may give linkpearls to the concierge to hand out. Players looking for a linkshell may then elect to receive one such linkpearl of their choosing.
      • Linkshell Concierge Locations and Functions
        Linkshell concierge locations and the style of linkshell they are in charge of are as follows.
        Location Style
        Western Adoulin (H-12) Casual battle content
        Western Adoulin (H-12) Assisting each other
        Eastern Adoulin (G-10) Hard-core battle content
        Eastern Adoulin (G-10) Everything
        Ru'Lude Gardens (G-9) Hard-core battle content
        Ru'Lude Gardens (G-9) Assisting each other
        Ru'Lude Gardens (G-9) Synthesis and trading
        Aht Urhgan Whitegate (H-9) Vanquishing HNMs
        Southern San d'Oria (G-10) Everything
        Southern San d'Oria (G-10) Meet new players
        Bastok Markets (F-9) Everything
        Bastok Markets (F-9) Meet new players
        Windurst Woods (H-10) Everything
        Windurst Woods (H-10) Meet new players
        * New styles and NPCs may be added after the November version update as needed.
      • Distributing Linkpearls
        Speak to a linkshell concierge while equipped with a linkpearl or pearlsack and select "I'd like to handout linkpearls." Answer a brief questionnaire with the following information and pay a 500 gil fee to have the concierge distribute linkpearls for you.
        • Language (JP, EN, other)
        • Number of applicants desired (1-10)
        • Main days of activity (Weekdays, weekends, specific days, every day)
        • Time zone (JST, PST/PDT, GMT, no setting)
        • Main time of activity (morning, afternoon, evening, night, wee hours of the morning)

        Additionally, equipping a linkpearl and pearl sack, selecting "show me recruiting linkshells," and picking the corresponding linkshell from the list will cancel that particular registration.

        * A linkshell may only be registered once per concierge.
        * A particular concierge can only register up to sixteen linkshells at a time. New registrations will push existing ones off the list in order of oldest to newest.
        * Maintenance and area restarts will delete all registrations from the corresponding concierges.
      • Speak to a linkshell concierge and select "Show me recruiting linkshells" to display a list available linkshells. Check the list of parameters the registrant has set and choose your desired linkshell.

        * Players may only receive one linkpearl per Vana'diel day.

    Known Issues

    • The screen will occasionally freeze when pressing 4 or 6 on the numpad to match up with the arrow that appears while fishing.

    • While undertaking the Records of Eminence objective "Magic Bursts (UC)," magic bursts activated via enfeebling magic or familiar special abilities does not count.
    • In the Seekers of Adoulin mission "Adbomination," camera locations and NPC movements in certain cutscenes are unnaturial.
    • In the Seekers of Adoulin mission "Blood for Blood," there are locations where NPC names do not display alongside their dialogues.
    • When the ninja job trait Daken activates while battling with unwavering ironclads in skirmishes taking place in Outer Ra'Kaznar [U], special abilities other than Turbine Cyclone are activated.
    • While equipped with a councilor's garb and opening the item section of the action command menu, councilor's garb is displayed in the item list.
    • Councilor's garbs erroneously display a defense value of 51 instead of the actual value of 1.
    • Using the beastmaster ability Call Beast after first using Unleash will cause the familiar summoned to display that it has zero TP.
      * This is a display bug only. The familiar will actually be summoned with 3,000 TP.
    • The Ex attribute is erroneously assigned to the charitoni sling.
    • The help text for the Vocane ring erroneously reads "Damage taken II -3%" instead of "Damage taken -7%."
    • The help text for the weapon Magesmasher erroneously reads "DMG:165" instead of "DMG:174."
    • The help text for the weapon Magesmasher +1 erroneously reads "DMG:166" instead of "DMG:175."
    • The stoneskin casting time attribute of the item Pukulatmuj +1 is lower than the display value in the help text.
    • In the "Tell" chat channel, when selecting someone to send a message to, holding down the right arrow key will not return to the first person.
