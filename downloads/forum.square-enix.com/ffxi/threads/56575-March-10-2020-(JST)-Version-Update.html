    Battle-related

    • New battle content, Odyssey, has been added.
      Odyssey is exploratory battle content in which players progress through an area of Walk of Echoes known as a Sheol.
      Materials you acquire in Sheol A can be used to augment equipment described later.
      • Requirement for Participation
        • Be in possession of a moglophone key item.
      • Enjoying the Content
        1. Examine the ??? in Rabao (G-6) to receive a moglophone.
        * You may receive another moglophone by interacting with the ??? again once twenty hours have passed.

        2. While in possession of a moglophone key item, examine the Veridical Conflux in Rabao (G-6) and select “Sheol A (Level: 119).”
        * Your moglophone will be consumed upon entry.
        • Parties must be between 1 and 6 people.
        • There is a time limit of 30 minutes.
      • Izzat
        Players will earn izzat, a special currency unique to Odyssey, as they vanquish monsters within.
        Izzat may be used to open the various chests and coffers found scattered throughout.
        * Izzat is shared amongst all party members and will be lost once your current Odyssey has ended.
      • Leaving Odyssey
        • Interact with the NPC found at the Sheol A exit.
        • Reach the time limit.
        • Use the moglophone temporary item.
          *The moglophone temporary item will be automatically granted to all players upon entering Odyssey.
    • Ambuscade has undergone the following additions and adjustments.
      • The foes in Normal and Intense Ambuscades have been swapped out.
        * As such, the corresponding Records of Eminence objectives have changed.
      • Certain items obtainable in exchange for hallmarks have been swapped out.
      • Certain items obtainable for total hallmarks have been swapped out.
      • Certain items obtainable in exchange for badges of gallantry have been swapped out.
    • Domain Invasion has undergone the following additions and adjustments.
      • Mireu’s spawn interval has been increased.
      • The content level now displays once Mireu spawns.
      • A domain point bonus is now given upon defeating Mireu.
        *This bonus is separate from the daily maximum.
      • The base number of domain points Mireu awards has been increased based on the number of successive times that Mireu has been defeated.
      Number of Successive KillsBase Domain Points
      0　10
      1　15
      2　20
      3　30
      4　40
      5+　50
      　Domain point limit increased to 100.
      • Mireu’s evasion and magic evasion have been greatly decreased.
      • Players will receive a status buff called “mobilization” when they participate in Domain Invasion.
      Players will not take any damage while this buff is active, but it will be removed when they take any enmity-generating action towards monsters.
      * The buff will not be removed if actions that do not generate enmity are taken.
      * Players will not receive any rewards while the buff is active.
      • An accuracy and magic accuracy bonus has been added to the “Awaken” buff.

    System-related

    • Adjustments have been made to high-tier battlefields occurring in Walk of Echoes [P] in order to decrease waiting time.
      Depending on instance congestion, players may be placed in either Walk of Echoes [P1] or Walk of Echoes [P2].
      * Which instance players will enter is automatically decided upon time of application.
      * The exact order of party entry may vary based on instance status.
      * The instance area name has no effect on the content itself.
