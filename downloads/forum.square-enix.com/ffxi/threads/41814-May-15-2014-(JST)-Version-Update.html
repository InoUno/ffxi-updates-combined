    Event-related

    • New Seekers of Adoulin missions have been added.
    • New quests have been added to Seekers of Adoulin areas.
    • A new area, Leafallia, has been added.
      Access to Leafallia is only granted to those who have progressed to a particular point in the newly-added Chapter 4 of the Seekers of Adoulin mission.
    • Geomancers of Rune Fencer may now obtain Ergon Weapons.
      Ergon Weapons are mythic-equivalent weapons for geomancers and rune fencers, and can be obtained by completing a special quest.
      • Undertaking the quests
        Geomancers and rune fencers have different prerequisites for undertaking their respective quests.
        • Geomancers
          • Have completed the quest “The Communion”
          • Be at the rank of “Legend” for all coalitions
        • Rune Fencers
          • Have completed the quest “Destiny’s Device”
          • Be at the rank of “Legend” for all coalitions
        • Notes
          Players may undertake both of these quests at once, but at a certain point players will be tasked with delivering certain items to a particular NPC. At this point, players are only able to deliver items for one of the two jobs.
          * Players will have to abandon their current quest in order to deliver items for the other quest, and all items previously delivered will be lost upon so doing.
      • NPCs offering the quests
        Players will be able to receive one of these quests by fulfilling the above prerequisites and speaking to the following NPCs.
        • Geomancer
          Nhili Uvolep in Eastern Adoulin (I-7)
        • Rune Fencers
          Gaddiux in Western Adoulin (J-10)
      • High-purity bayld
        Throughout the quests, players will need to obtain items known as “high-purity bayld.”
        • Obtaining high-purity bayld
          High-purity bayld can be obtained through all manner of reives.
          • Obtained from Colonization and Lair Reives
            Players will sometimes receive high-purity bayld by reaching a certain minimum evaluation through participating in a reive.
            Colonization reives are more likely to provide high-purity bayld than lair reives.
            * Even after players have met the minimum evaluation level to receive high-purity bayld, the odds that they will receive it will continue to increase in line with increases in evaluation.
          • Obtained from Wildskeeper Reives
            Players will always receive high-purity bayld by successfully vanquishing a Naakual as long as they receive a certain level of evaluation.
          • Purchased from Runje Desaali
            Players may purchase high-purity bayld for 10,000 bayld from Runje Desaali in Eastern Adoulin (J-10) by selecting the appropriate dialogue option.
          • Obtained from Soul Pyres
            There is a slight chance that players will receive high-purity bayld when they open a Soul Pyre containing bayld.
    • New alter egos have been added.
      Read on for details.
    • The following additions and adjustments have been made to Records of Eminence.
      • New objectives have been added.
      • Capacity points, abbreviated as “CAP,” have been added to the list of rewards for certain objectives
        * Capacity points will only be awarded to those in possession of the key item “job breaker.”
      • The respawn time of the NM “Bloodsucker,” the target of a certain Subjugation objective, has been reduced.
      • Copper A.M.A.N. vouchers have undergone the following adjustments.
        • Players are now able to specify how many vouchers they wish to redeem at once.
        • Players may now redeem vouchers for 1,000 Ballista points.
    • The maximum coalition rank has been increased to eight, and additional coalition services have been made available.

    System-related

    • The Mog Wardrobe, a new form of equipment storage, has been added.
      Your Mog Wardrobe can hold up to 80 pieces of equipment separate from your inventory, and items can be stored or removed at any time.
      • Using Your Mog Wardrobe
        Select “Mog Wardrobe” from the “View House” sections of the main menu.
        * There are no requirements for accessing your Mog Wardrobe.
      • Equipping Items from your Mog Wardrobe
        Items stored in your Mog Wardrobe can be equipped directly from the “Equipment” section of the main menu.
      • Changing equipment
        Selecting “Storage Options” from the Equipment option in the main menu will let you switch between your inventory and Mog Wardrobe.
        • Regarding the /equip command
          　
          When equipping items from your Mog Wardrobe via the /equip command, you must specify from which storage space you would like to take the item.
        • Designating a bag
          Insert the appropriate value after the equipment name as follows:
          Ex.: /equip main Onion Sword 1
          Corresponding values:
          • No value specified
            The game will first search your inventory for the specified equipment, then searing your Mog Wardrobe. It will equip the items if found.
          • 0
            Equip from your inventory.
          • 1
            Equip from your Mog Wardrobe.
    • New home points have been established in the following areas.
      Caedarva Mire (E-9) / Bhaflau Thickets (I-9)
    • “Misc. 2” and “Alchemy 2” categories have been added to the auction house.

    • The help text for the weapon "Homestead Cesti" erroneously displays "Occasionally attacks twice" in place of the correct attribute ""TP Bonus"+1000"
      The help text for the bard job point category "Minuet Effect" misleadingly implies that the physical attack increase is only gained by the bard. The bonus in fact applies to all allies under the effects of Minuet.
    • The damage value for the weapon “Bestas Bane” is incorrect.
